Much of the tasks performed with the computing medium today involves running complex calculations, collaborative editing and production of tech-enabled art. However, with newer modalities of computing entering the large-scale user aspirations, computing can acquire newer purposes. Chunks of data gathering, organization , encoding and decoding are activities whose agencies are being shifted or are at the verge of being shifted to automated systems.

There’s been a significant growth in aversion towards prolonged use of flat-screen devices owing to several factors. Usage of some software(s) on mobile and desktop computers have become causations for diagnosis of addiction of certain kinds. In the age of information, as referred to popularly, people view technology as an antidote to pleasures of the natural environment. Although there are several reasons rooted in user demographics, contexts and general state of well-being that can be attributed to these perceptions, the design of technology has fundamentally differentiated itself from the ecosystem we’re ‘meant’ to thrive in.
