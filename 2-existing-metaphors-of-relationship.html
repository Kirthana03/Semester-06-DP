2 Existing metaphors of relationship

Metaphors have served a great purpose in the history of inventions in direct and in indirect manners. In this case, the manipulation of flat-screen for dealing with context-specific information has settled deep-down in the mental models of software technology-users. Pen and paper metaphor has long influenced the notions of creativity with computing mediums. The combined experience of pixels ( a light-emitting source) and material (a light-reflecting source) presents several opportunities for purposes and abilities of computing.

What metaphors could computing adopt eventually?
